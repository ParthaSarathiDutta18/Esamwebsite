The thrill of solving problems and creating new solutions keeps me constantly learning and adapting, and I’m excited to be part of a field that’s always pushing boundaries and shaping the future.

Music

Music is my ultimate source of inspiration and creativity. It’s not just about melodies and rhythms, but the emotions and stories they convey. Whether it’s the mind-bending, experimental sounds of Pink Floyd, the revolutionary vibes of The Beatles, the raw intensity of Nirvana’s grunge anthems, or the poetic depth of Bob Dylan’s lyrics, each artist brings something unique that fuels my love for music. From the introspective, philosophical themes of Pink Floyd to the rebellious spirit of Nirvana, and the timeless, socially-conscious messages of Dylan and The Beatles, their music has shaped my view of the world and continues to inspire me every day.

Poetry

Poetry is where language becomes a canvas, and every word paints an emotion, a moment, or a story. It's the art of expressing the inexpressible, whether through a rhythmic verse, a quiet haiku, or a deep, sprawling metaphor. Like the lyrics of a song, poetry captures the raw essence of the human experience, inviting us to feel, think, and reflect in ways that go beyond the ordinary. From the delicate beauty of love to the complexities of pain and joy, poetry allows us to explore the world with a fresh perspective, making it a powerful, personal form of creative expression.
